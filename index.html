I'm not here to sell you signals or a magic system. I'm here to be there for you and help you get better, and understand exactly how to become a successful trader.

Outside of trading, I have a degree in Software Engineering from Florida Tech. I run a trading community where we all push each other to improve every day, and I love to work out.

What to Expect

What we'll cover together

Every session is tailored to you, but these are the areas of trading that I'll probably cover most, depending on where you're at. We'll hit the parts and areas that you and I see fit.

01

Initial Assessment

Before we dive in, I'll figure out exactly where you're at. Your experience, your struggles, your goals. This is how I know where to focus your time.

02

Mindset & Psychology

The mental game is the most overlooked aspect in trading. It is where 99% of traders fail, and why most traders lose. We'll build the discipline, emotional control, and that trader's state of mind you need.

03

Technical Analysis and Charting

I'll show you how to read the chart like a book and know what you're looking at instead of guessing. This is the foundation of everything, and how you are choosing your trades.

04

ICT, Markets, and Smart Money Concepts

Understanding things like Liquidity, market structure, order flow, session bias, etc., is essential. Knowing how the market actually moves, why things are happening... Not just what lagging indicators say.

05

Execution & Performance

The only thing you can control is your execution and the mindset you bring. This is a hard concept for us because our brain always wants to control every scenario we encounter. We'll get this on point.

06

Risk Management

Stop losses, position sizing, personal rules, trade checklists. The boring stuff that traders like to neglect, but what keeps you in the game long enough to let you actually trade successfully and profitably.

07

Trading Strategies

Everyone sees the charts differently, so no one will have the exact same thought process for taking a trade, though they can be very similar. But I will go through my strategy and other strategies that actually work, if needed, regardless of the markets and timeframes you're trading.

08

Application

Whether it's a simulated, live, or funded account, I'll help you practice and apply your skills, and guide you to success when you trade live
