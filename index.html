Not at all. Whether you've never placed a trade or you're already funded, I tailor every session to where you're at. Beginners get a full game plan from scratch — experienced traders get targeted strategy refinement.

Bring your charts, your questions, or specific setups you want to review. If you're a beginner, just show up with an open mind — I'll handle the rest. Having TradingView open is helpful but not required.

Use code FIRST1 when booking your first 1-hour session to get $150 off (pay $100 instead of $250). It's only available for first-time bookings on the 1-hour session.
