About

A little about my story

I've been trading for over 3 and a half years. I started as a freshman in college, and over those first 2-3 years, I proceeded to lose all the money to my name. Tens of thousands of dollars, just gone with nothing to show for it. This definitely led me to some dark places, but one thing never changed...the belief that I was going to figure out how to become a profitable trader.

I proceeded to lock myself in my room for the majority of my days, learning, experimenting, losing, winning, and finding how to truly become successful in the trading space. After about 3 years of nothing to show for it, something in my mind clicked, and I finally started to see results. This is when I got my first-ever prop firm payout, and my live accounts stopped draining to 0. Though I had no results for so long, I trusted gods plan, never gave up, and now get to live the live I always wanted to live, and do what I truly love.

That's what trading really is...freedom. I trade for about 2 hours in the morning, and the rest of my day is mine. No boss, no commute, no 9-5. Just me, my charts, and a life I built on my own terms. That's what I want to help you build too.

I've been through pretty much every phase of this game now, whether it's the blowups, the breakthroughs, the mental battles with yourself, etc. That experience is what I bring to you. I'm not here to sell you signals or a magic system. I'm here to be there for you and help you get better, and understand exactly how to become a successful trader.

Outside of trading, I have a degree in Software Engineering from Florida Tech. I run a trading community where we all push each other to improve every day, and I love to work out.
